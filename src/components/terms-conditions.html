1. Eligibility Criteria

  1. The JSC Godfather Offer is available exclusively to clients who have formally engaged JSC Property Investments as their buyer's agent for the acquisition of investment properties, as evidenced by a duly executed Engagement Form.

  1. The JSC Godfather Offer includes:

3. JSC Property Investments Priority Properties Recommendation

  1. The Offer applies solely to properties selected and recommended by JSC Property Investments, following our due diligence and property selection process, ensuring alignment with the client's Property Action Plan. Properties that are independently sourced by the client or recommended by third parties shall not be covered by the Offer.
  2. For the purposes of these T&Cs, "Priority Properties" refer to those identified by JSC as having the highest potential to contribute to the client's long-term goals, as outlined in the property Investment Report.

    2. The client retains the right to select properties from JSC Property Investments' recommendations outside of the Priority Properties list but such properties will not qualify for the Offer.

  1. The performance of the selected investment property will be evaluated in relation to its contribution toward the objectives outlined in the client's Property Action Plan. JSC Guarantee Terms and Conditions (T&Cs)

These Terms and Conditions ("T&Cs") govern your participation in the JSC Guarantee (the "Guarantee") provided by JSC Property Investments ("JSC," "we," "us," or "our"). By participating in the Guarantee, you ("Client," "you," or "your") agree to be bound by these T&Cs.

1. Eligibility Criteria

  1. The Guarantee shall apply exclusively to properties purchased through JSC Property Investments, provided that the purchase price of the property meets either of the qualifications below:
    1. The property is valued at no less than $800,000, with a capital growth threshold of $100,000 within 12 months following settlement; or
    2. The property is valued at or below $799,999.99, with a capital growth threshold equal to 10% of the purchase price within 12 months following settlement.
  2. The Guarantee shall apply solely to properties purchased with the intention of investment, whether for capital growth or cash flow purposes, and shall not extend to owner-occupied properties.

2. JSC Property Investments Priority Properties Recommendation

  1. The Guarantee shall be valid solely for Priority Properties selected and recommended by JSC Property Investments, following our due diligence and property selection process. Properties that are independently sourced by the client or recommended by third parties shall not be eligible for the Guarantee.
  2. For the purposes of these Terms and Conditions, "Priority Properties" refer to those properties identified by JSC Property Investments as having the highest potential for capital growth.
    1. JSC Property Investments endeavours to inform the client whether the recommended property is part of the Priority Properties list through the Investment Reports, which will be provided to the client from time to time during the property search phase.
    2. The client retains the right to select properties from JSC Property Investments' recommendations outside of the Priority Properties list.

3. Verification and Appraisal

  1. Within two weeks following the conclusion of the 12-month period after purchase, the property's capital growth will be assessed based on independent, third-party appraisals recommended by JSC Property Investments. The identity of the third-party appraisers will be communicated by JSC to the client in writing.
  2. To qualify for the Guarantee, the valuation must demonstrate a capital growth of less than:
    1. $100,000 from the original purchase price for properties valued at or above $800,000; or
    2. 10% of the original purchase price for properties valued at or below $799,999.99.
  3. If the capital growth, as determined following the 12-month period, exceeds the aforementioned thresholds, the Guarantee shall terminate with immediate effect.

    1. In such circumstances, JSC Property Investments will notify the client in writing of its decision and, where applicable, provide reasonable evidence to demonstrate the material impact of the adverse economic conditions on the property market.

5. Client Obligations

  1. The client shall make reasonable efforts to adhere to the Terms outlined in the Engagement Form. In the event that the client breaches any of the terms set forth in the Engagement Form, JSC reserves the right to rescind the Guarantee without prior notice.
