JSC Godfather Offer Terms and Conditions

The JSC Godfather Offer includes personalised property recommendations, strategic advisory services, and access to our proprietary ‘Capital Compounder’ strategy, which is tailored to help you generate substantial passive income and build wealth in the long term.

1. Eligibility Criteria

  • The offer is available only to clients who have formally engaged JSC as their buyer’s agent through a signed Engagement Form.
  • Clients must complete a comprehensive Fact Find and receive a Property Action Plan from JSC.
  • Clients must be Australian residents or legally authorized to invest in Australian property.

2. Offer Description

  • Includes identification and recommendation of investment properties aligned with the Property Action Plan.
  • Includes strategic advisory services based on the "Capital Compounder" strategy.
  • JSC makes no guarantees of specific financial outcomes due to market variables.
  • The offer is only valid for properties acquired through JSC.

3. Priority Properties Recommendation

  • Applies only to properties selected through JSC’s due diligence process.
  • JSC will identify Priority Properties through Investment Reports.
  • Clients may choose non-priority properties but they won’t qualify for the offer.

4. Performance Assessment

  • Performance is evaluated against the Property Action Plan goals.
  • JSC will provide annual or otherwise agreed Progress Reports.
  • Target outcomes are illustrative and not guaranteed.

5. Market Conditions as a Factor

In the case of significant economic changes, JSC may review, adjust, or suspend the offer with written notice and supporting evidence.

6. Client Obligations

  • Clients must follow the Engagement Form and Property Action Plan recommendations.
  • Clients bear all ownership costs.
  • Non-compliance may void the offer without notice.

7. Offer Coverage Period

Commences at property settlement and continues through the duration of the Property Action Plan.

8. Exclusions

  • Does not apply to owner-occupied or altered/refinanced/sold properties during the plan period without JSC's approval.
  • Losses due to client mismanagement or deviation from the plan are excluded.

9. Limitation of Liability

JSC is not liable for indirect or consequential losses. Liability is limited to fees paid as outlined in the Engagement Form.

10. One-Time Offer

The offer applies once per client unless otherwise stated by JSC.

11. Contact Information

Inquiries can be directed to info@jscpropertyinvestments.com.au. Responses expected within two business days.

12. Governing Law

Governed by Australian law, with Queensland courts having non-exclusive jurisdiction.

13. Separability

If any part is found unenforceable, the rest remains valid and binding.

JSC Guarantee Terms and Conditions

JSC guarantees a minimum of $100,000 in capital growth within 12 months or waives its Engagement and Success Fee for your next property, subject to these terms:

1. Eligibility Criteria

  • Applies to properties purchased through JSC that meet the following:
    • Valued ≥ $800,000 with ≥ $100,000 capital growth, or
    • Valued ≤ $799,999.99 with ≥ 10% capital growth.
  • Only applies to investment properties, not owner-occupied ones.

2. Priority Properties Recommendation

  • Guarantee is valid only for Priority Properties recommended by JSC.
  • JSC informs clients about Priority Property status via Investment Reports.

3. Verification and Appraisal

  • Capital growth is assessed via third-party appraisal after 12 months.
  • Valuation must show less than the growth thresholds to qualify for the guarantee.
  • If thresholds are met/exceeded, the guarantee ends.

4. Market Conditions as a Factor

JSC may adjust or suspend the guarantee in response to material economic changes and will notify the client in writing if this occurs.

5. Client Obligations

Clients must adhere to the Engagement Form. Breaches may void the guarantee without notice.

6. Guarantee Claim Entitlements

If the capital growth is not achieved, JSC waives the Engagement and Success Fee for the next purchase. All other costs remain the client’s responsibility.

7. Guarantee Coverage Period

Starts at settlement and lasts for 12 months.

8. One-Time Offer

Applies once and not to future purchases unless otherwise stated.

9. Exclusions

  • Void if the property is sold, refinanced, or materially altered within 12 months without JSC’s approval.
  • “Materially altered” means any change that negatively affects the property’s value or marketability.

10. Applicable Law

Governed by Australian law with Queensland courts having non-exclusive jurisdiction.

11. Separability

If any provision is found invalid, the remainder stays enforceable.
